About Us

This is a platform, where you'll find electronics products using Arduino interface, IOT(internet of things) smart circuits, and all types of circuit designing and prototyping with exclusively affordable prices. Our core vision is to help the engineering students for their projects to meet the deadline. We founded e-INNOVATORS with one goal in mind: providing a high-quality, smart, and reliable electronic circuit to the students. Our passion for excellence has driven us from the beginning and continues to drive us into the future.
