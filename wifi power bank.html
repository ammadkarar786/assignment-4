How to make a free Power Bank for your Wifi Router! | Wifi connection ease!

by Qunber Shah

Posted on April 13, 2020

  • spdt relay
  • 1 step up module xl6009
  • 1 schokey diode
  • 2 3.7v li-ion battery
  • veroboard
  • 2 dc jack

How you can make a free power bank at home for your wifi just like an electricity inverter. Nowadays everyone is spending lots of time on mobile and laptop due to the lockdown. The education of students is conducted online, marketing(selling and purchasing are done online more than before), in these circumstances due to the loss of electricity or any electrical issue at your place or area, no one would appreciate problems in their wifi network. But don't worry we have got you covered, in this video, we have a complete tutorial of how you can make a power bank or an inverter, which turns on automatically in case of any power failure and your wifi router continues to work smoothly. This power bank is chargeable and most importantly it turns off and on automatically in case of a power failure and doesn't affect the working of your wi-fi router even for a second during the switch of the power supply and the power bank even for a second.
